## SCENE 1 — "The leak you can't see" (8s)

**🎬 VISUAL PROMPT (paste into Hailuo/Veo):**
> A manufacturing plant floor viewed from above, machines running, but faint glowing amber particles drift up and dissipate from several machines — a visual metaphor for money and value quietly leaking away. Serious, cinematic mood, controlled amber warning glow against cool graphite industrial tones. Slow overhead drift. Photorealistic, subtle volumetric haze.

**🎥 CAMERA / MOTION:** Slow top-down drift over the plant; amber particles rise and fade as we pass each machine.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER (VO):** "Every plant leaks money it never sees — unplanned breakdowns, repeat repairs, missed permits, wasted energy."

**🔤 ON-SCREEN TEXT (post):** `Unplanned repairs cost 4–5× more` → `Where is your budget going?`

**🎵 AUDIO / SFX:** Low tense drone, faint machine hum, a slow ticking clock underneath. Somber.

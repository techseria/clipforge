# TEASER — Single 8-Second Cut

**Purpose:** One standalone 8-second clip for the top of a feed (LinkedIn, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp status, paid pre-roll). Built to stop the scroll and land ONE idea: *your plant now has a manager who never sleeps.*
**Length:** **1 scene × 8s.** No merging required — this is a single generation.

> Read `README.md` → **STYLE BIBLE** first. Append the global style tag:
> `cinematic, photorealistic, volumetric light, smooth motion, shallow depth of field, high detail, 16:9, no on-screen text, no captions`
> For Reels/Shorts, render **9:16** instead — see `vertical-9x16-guide.md`.

## THE 8-SECOND SCENE

**🎬 VISUAL PROMPT (paste into Hailuo/Veo):**
> A dark, quiet manufacturing plant at night. In one smooth continuous move, a calm teal-blue wave of light sweeps across the whole floor — as it passes, idle machines light up with soft glowing status halos, a robotic arm and conveyor come alive, and holographic UI cards ("Work order created," "Failure predicted," "SLA on track") bloom briefly and fade. The camera glides forward through the plant toward a glass control room glowing electric blue, then eases to a calm stop on empty premium negative space for a logo. Cool graphite industrial tones with electric-blue #3B82F6 and soft-purple #8B5CF6 glow, a hint of success green. Cinematic, photorealistic, one seamless motion.

**🎥 CAMERA / MOTION:** One continuous forward glide (dolly-in) synced to the teal light wave; settle to a still end frame in the last 1.5s.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER (VO) — ~17 words, calm and intriguing:**
> "What if your plant had a manager who never slept, never missed a permit, never let it break?"

**🔤 ON-SCREEN TEXT (post) — keep it to 2 beats:**
- Beat 1 (0–5s): `Meet MIRA.`
- Beat 2 (5–8s, over the end frame): `MIRA` glyph + `Your Digital Facility Manager.` → tiny CTA `↗ link in bio` / `Learn more →`

**🎵 AUDIO / SFX:** Starts quiet and tense, one soft rising synth swell that peaks as the control room reveals; a single confident brand chime + soft "whoosh" on the end card. On Veo 3 generate this natively; on Hailuo add in post.

### Variations you can generate from this same scene
- **Hook-question cut (above):** best for cold audiences / paid.
- **Result cut:** swap VO to *"3 A.M. A motor starts to fail. MIRA fixes it before your shift even begins."* — keep visuals identical.
- **Silent/social autoplay cut:** no VO; rely entirely on the on-screen text beats (most feeds autoplay muted). Keep the music.

### Tips
- Put `Meet MIRA.` on screen within the first 1s — feeds decide in ~1.5s.
- Keep total text short; 9:16 crops the sides, so center everything.
- End on the wordmark held for at least 1.5s so viewers can read the CTA.

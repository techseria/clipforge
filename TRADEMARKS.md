# Trademark Policy

> How you may (and may not) use the ClipForge name, logo, and related marks.

The **ClipForge** name and logo are trademarks of **Techseria** (https://techseria.com). This trademark policy is designed to allow the open-source community to use the marks in ways that support and grow the project, while protecting Techseria's rights and reputation.

## What's a "mark"?

"ClipForge Marks" refers to:

- The word "ClipForge" (and any domain, subdomain, or social handle that includes it)
- The ClipForge logo (any version, including the ⏧ glyph in the README)
- The "ClipForge" wordmark / typographic treatment
- Any product name, slogan, or tagline that includes "ClipForge"

## ✅ You can (without permission)

You **may** use the ClipForge Marks, **without** asking us, in the following ways:

### Referring to the project
- ✅ Stating that you use, deploy, or contribute to ClipForge
- ✅ Linking to the official repo (https://github.com/techseria/clipforge) or website (https://techseria.com)
- ✅ Mentioning ClipForge in articles, blog posts, talks, conference slides, books
- ✅ Using the name in your resume/CV ("I contributed to ClipForge")

### In your fork
- ✅ Naming your fork something like "ClipForge for Education" or "ClipForge XYZ edition" **as long as the name is clearly disambiguated** (e.g. includes your own name, organization, or distinguishing suffix)
- ✅ Using the name in your fork's repository description
- ✅ Using the logo as your fork's icon

### In your community
- ✅ Naming a community "ClipForge Users" or "ClipForge Community"
- ✅ Hosting a "ClipForge Meetup"
- ✅ Naming a YouTube series "ClipForge Tutorials" (with a clear link to the official repo)
- ✅ Naming a Discord/Slack "ClipForge Community"

### Educational use
- ✅ Using the logo in a presentation about ClipForge
- ✅ Using the name in a tutorial or course ("How to deploy ClipForge")
- ✅ Translating the docs and naming your translation "ClipForge Docs (Japanese)"

## ⚠️ You should ask us first

These uses require **explicit written permission** from <trademarks@techseria.com>:

- 🔶 Using the name in a **commercial product name** (e.g. "ClipForge Pro" as a paid SaaS)
- 🔶 Using the logo in **paid advertising** (Google Ads, etc.)
- 🔶 Using the name in a **domain name** (other than `your-org.com/clipforge`)
- 🔶 Using the logo on **physical merchandise** for sale (T-shirts, stickers, etc.)
- 🔶 Using the name in a **conference or event name** (e.g. "ClipForge Conference 2027")
- 🔶 Co-branding or suggesting Techseria endorsement

We rarely refuse; we usually just want to know about it. **Email us — most requests are approved within a few days.**

## ❌ You must NOT

The following are **never allowed** without express written permission:

- ❌ Using the ClipForge name or logo in a way that suggests Techseria endorses your product or service
- ❌ Using the ClipForge name in a way that is defamatory, obscene, infringing, or otherwise illegal
- ❌ Modifying the logo (changing colors, proportions, adding effects) — use the official SVG/PNG as-is
- ❌ Registering a domain name, social media account, or trademark that includes "ClipForge" without permission
- ❌ Using the ClipForge name in a way that confuses users about which software is official
- ❌ Selling merchandise with the ClipForge name/logo (other than the explicit permission case above)
- ❌ Using the ClipForge name to disparage Techseria or the open-source community

## When in doubt, ask

If you're unsure whether your use is permitted, email **<trademarks@techseria.com>** with a short description. We respond within 5 business days.

## Policy changes

Techseria may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced on the [Techseria blog](https://techseria.com/blog) at least 30 days before they take effect.

## Disputes

If you believe someone is misusing the ClipForge marks, please email <trademarks@techseria.com> with details. Techseria will investigate and take appropriate action.
